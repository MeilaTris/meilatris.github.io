Designation & Purpose

The Obsidian-class Starship was commissioned to address repeated failures of conventional platforms to adequately observe, document, and survive long-duration consequence-bearing missions.

Unlike exploratory, diplomatic, or frontline combat vessels, the Obsidian-class operates where outcomes remain unresolved, historical accountability is required, and withdrawal introduces unacceptable loss of information.

The Obsidian-class is not an exploration vessel. It is an oversight platform.

Design Philosophy

The Obsidian-class is built around a single doctrinal principle: Truth requires more than one witness.

The class is intended to deploy in parallel units to minimize technological bias while enabling independent analytical divergence through separate crews and command interpretation.

Operational Doctrine · Parallel Oversight

Obsidian-class vessels deploy as parallel witnesses. Each maintains independent analysis, separate command interpretation, and distinct mission logs. Discrepancy is preserved. Consensus is not required.
