Briefing

We exist after certainty fails.

Starfleet believed the Ancient Fleet was a myth—redacted history, dormant hulls, corrupted records. But fragments persist across the quadrant, and their consequences are unfolding in the present. The Obsidian Accord observes, evaluates, and responds when history refuses to stay buried.

Some missions don’t end cleanly. Someone has to stay and take notes.
